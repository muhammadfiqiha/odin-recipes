Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is classic Italian dish that has known for its layers of rich flavors and comforting textures. Lasagna is a layered pasta dish that traditionally consists of wide, flat pasta sheets, meat sauce, bechamel sauce, and melted cheese. Lasagna has gained popularity and a cherished place in the heart of food enthusiasts around the world.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings (fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper)
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese (Parmesan, Mozarella, or Ricotta)
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
